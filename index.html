About this game

Created By: Kevin Gitaari

Ping pong will have to count from zero to the number that you have provided:

Here are the RULES of how the game is played:

  • Numbers Divisible by 3 will be replaced with "ping "

  • Numbers divisible by 5 will be replaced with "pong "

  • Numbers divisible by both 3 and 5 will be replaced as "pingpong "

  • The rest of the numbers will be displayed as RAW Numbers
